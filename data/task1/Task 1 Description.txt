Task 1: e-News2015 categorization

This e-News2015 dataset is collected from eight newspapers: The New Zealand Herald (www.nzherald.co.nz), The Australian (www.theaustralian.com.au), The Press (www.stuff.co.nz), Yahoo News (news.yahoo.com), BBC (www.bbc.co.uk), The New York Times (www.nytimes.com), The Independent (www.independent.co.uk), The Times (www.timesonline.co.uk) and Herald Sun (www.heraldsun.com.au/), on five topics of business, entertainment, sport, technology, and travel respectively. Each document of the dataset is labelled manually by skimming over the text and determining the category. In the provided data files, each document is formatted as one line pure text with the first character as the class label (for training data), punctuations and stop words removed in advance.
